No Comment A Cappella

Being a beatboxer, it was only natural for me to want to see what A Cappella was about at university. Let's just say that it was a humbling and an extremely fun experience to make music and compete against other groups. Check out our song with me on vocal percussion. Please note that it isn't a lot like Pitch Perfect, it's quite impossible for 15 people to freestyle and sound good at the same time.
